Alasdair Monk

Digital Product Designer, Developer & Strategist

Today, I'm a Principal Product Designer at Salesforce working on our Heroku product and towards making developer experiences better.

Prior to this, I was the Head of Design at GoCardless where I worked for three years on democratising payments for small businesses.

I'm keenly interested in the intersection of design & code and most of my writing reflects this. I like big ideas and hard projects and making something from nothing.
